Isiah Martin - Network Engineer at First National Bank of Omaha

Isiah's Career Path:

Over the next few months, I submitted my application and interviewed for an IT Internship. I accepted the position in spring of 2016, and hit the ground running.

During my internship, my main focus was providing a POC of a new automation tool named QA Wizard. During that time, I was also exposed to programming, networking, and project management. With a background in Networking, I was very excited to get hands on experience of how networks work in the real world.
