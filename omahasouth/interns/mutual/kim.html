Kim Duong - IT Intern at Mutual of Omaha Insurance

Kim's Story:
After taking an undergraduate course in MIS University of Nebraska Omaha (UNO), she felt strongly about majoring in Management Information Systems and decided to pursue graduate studies right after earning undergraduate degree in General Studies with a concentration in Healthcare Administration in May 2017. With a background in healthcare, she had an opportunity to work as graduate research assistant on a healthcare analytics project during summer 2018 at College of Information Systems & Technology which sparked her interest in a career working with data.

In Fall 2018, Kim was offered the opportunity to work as an IT intern at Mutual of Omaha. She has been working at Mutual of Omaha since January 2019. Currently, she is finishing up her last semester of graduate school and will be graduating in May 2019.
