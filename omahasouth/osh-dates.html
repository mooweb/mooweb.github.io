Key Project Dates

The following opportunities are open to Omaha South High School students only. Thank you for your interest!

Date/Timeframe Milestone Meeting Times Location Overview Link Deadline
April 26, 2019 Project Applications Due n/a n/a As a reminder, the team captain must submit a single application for each team. Also remember to review the project requirements for information on the expectations for the research portion of this project. Thank you for your interest in working with us to use your technical skills to work on a community challenge/issue that is important to you. We are excited to see what you come up with! April 26 - May 1, 2019 Project Selection Committee Review Period n/a n/a The project selection review committee will use the research section of the project requirements to determine which groups will be selected to move forward. All team captains will receive a notification of their selection status by May 1. A formal announcement will be made during the Tech Career Exploration Fair at AIM Institute on May 2, 2019. n/a Notifcation Deadline: May 1, 2019
May 10, 2019 Bring Your A+ Game Workshop 8:30a - 2:55p Omaha South High School During this required session led by Metropolitan Community College in collaboration with Heartland Workforce Solutions, students selected to participate in the project portion of this initiative will learn essential soft skills for the workplace. This training session will be held at Omaha South High School. Skills students will learn include:
  • Professionalism
  • Relational Skills
  • Conflict Resolution
  • Taking Direction
 Registration will be available on May 1, 2019 May 5, 2019
May 6 - June 27, 2019 Community Project Development Period TBD depending on team needs and mentor coordination Skype, Google Hangout, conference call - team will decide what works best to achieve project outcomes During this timeframe, students will work with assigned mentors to achieve project outcomes. More information on the project structure and requirements is available here. n/a Project Deadline: June 27, 2019
June 28, 2019 Demo Day Presentations 6:00pm - 8:00pm TBD During the final event for the summer pilot program, student teams will present their products to an audience of family, friends, teachers, mentors, etc. Each presentation will be judged by a panel of Greater Omaha based community leaders. Projects will be judged based on the following criteria:
  • Ambition of project
  • Innovation of solution
  • Quality of implementation
  • Quality of presentation
  • Team collaboration
The winning project team will receive a $500 stipend, 2nd place: $250, 3rd place: $100. All students who complete the entire project based on the requirements listed here who also present on Demo Day will receive a $250 stipend. 		Project submission and attendee registration links for demo day presentations will be available on June 1, 2019. Registration for demo day will close on June 27, 2019.
