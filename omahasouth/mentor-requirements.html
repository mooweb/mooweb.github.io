Volunteer Requirements

Overview Volunteer Roles Time Commitment Register
Thank you for your interest in participating in the Omaha South Pilot Summer Tech program sponsored by Mutual of Omaha Insurance. This page includes volunteer requirements for volunteer mentors.

Volunteer Types

Two types of volunteers will be required for the program. Details follow:

Volunteer Coach

Each Volunteer Coach will be assigned to a single team for the duration of the project. The Volunteer Coach may not possess all of the skills required to support the team's product development goals and will coordinate with volunteer mentors to provide support as needed. Individuals in this role will assist their assigned team's student captain with creating and maintaining a project schedule, schedule weekly meetings with the team to help them identify next steps and clear roadblocks, and complete weekly performance feedback questionnaires for each student on their team. Volunteer Coaches should serve in a project management related role at Mutual of Omaha Insurance..

Volunteer Mentor

Volunteer mentors will receive support requests from the volunteer coach. Time and skill level requirements of volunteers in this role will vary based on the needs of the team.

Skills Requirements

  • Front-End Web Development
  • Database Management
  • User Experience / User Interface Design
  • Mobile Application Development
  • Research Methods
  • Technical Writing
  • Presentation Preparation

Dates

This project will run from May 10 - June 28, 2019. During this timeframe, student teams and volunteer mentors will meet online or offsite at Omaha South High to address project support issues as they come up. Additionally, each team will be assigned a single mentor for the duration of the initiative. The assigned mentor will serve as the single point of contact for the team and will help the team identify resources within Mutual of Omaha to help them address issues as they come up.

Weekly Feedback Survey

On a weekly basis, each team's assigned mentor will complete a short questionnaire which will include the following questions for all student team members:
  • Did the team member provide valuable contributions to the project this week? (Yes/No)
  • If yes/no: What is the key highlight from this week?
  • If no: What is the one area of opportunity the student should focus on next week?
Survey results will be shared with faculty and program administrators.
